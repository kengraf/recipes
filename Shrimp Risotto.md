#  Shrimp Risotto

**Prep** 15  
**Cook** 60  
**Makes** 4

###  Ingredients

  *  **1** lb shrimp
  *  **7** cups water
  *   **2** bay leaves
  *   **1** tblsp course black pepper
  *   **2** tblp butter
  *   **1** onion chopper fine
  *   **1** fennel bulb, cored and chopped fine
  *   **1/8** tsp baking soda
  *   **1** cup arborio rice
  *   **3/4** cup dry white wine
  *   **1/2** cup parmesan cheese
  *   **1/4** cup fresh chives
  *   **1/2** tsp lemon zest
  *   **1** tblsp lemon juice
